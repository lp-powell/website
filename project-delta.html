74' CL350
Repairing and improving a classic

BACKGROUND.... The Honda CL350 is an air cooled, carbureted twin cylinder 350 cc scrambler motorcycle. It is about as simple as an adventure bike gets, with no electronic controls other than blinkers, headlights and the speedometer and tach. With this machine being over 50 years old there are some things that need improving. The blinker relay was replaced with a new one, allowing for the blinkers and headlight to be replaced with LED bulbs. The LED bults also need some diodes wired into the system to work. A new regulator and rectifier were installed along with a new battery. Everything else on the bike is OEM apart from the tires and exhaust tips. Many components, like the brake drums, have been replaced with new ones.
